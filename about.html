Who we are

Simizi is Canada's national employment service, available as a website. We help immigrants find work and plan their careers, and we make it easier for employers to recruit and hire across the Country.

Our story and what we do

On Simizi, We've come a long way from the days of paper job postings pinned to jobs. We draw on a hundred years of experience helping You succeed in the labour market. we leading source of jobs and labour market information in Canada.
